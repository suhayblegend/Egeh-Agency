Dua Exchange

Prayer Times, Quran & Islamic Resources

Client Dua Exchange
Year 2024
Services Full-Stack Development
Visit Live Site
01

Project Overview

Dua Exchange is a comprehensive Islamic platform designed to help Muslims stay connected to their faith through accurate prayer times, Quran recitation, and a curated collection of authentic duas and supplications.

We created a beautiful, user-friendly platform that combines modern design with spiritual purpose. The platform provides location-based prayer times, audio Quran recitations with translations, and a searchable database of duas for every occasion.

5 Daily Prayer Times
114 Surahs Available
100+ Authentic Duas
02

The Challenge

Accurate Prayer Times

Prayer times vary by location and calculation method. Users needed reliable, location-specific timings.

Authentic Content

Users needed verified, authentic Islamic content from reliable scholarly sources.

Mobile Accessibility

Most users access spiritual content on their phones throughout the day.

Audio Integration

Users wanted to listen to Quran recitations with clear audio and easy navigation.

03

Our Solution

01

Precise Prayer Time Calculator

We integrated multiple calculation methods (Muslim World League, ISNA, etc.) with geolocation to provide accurate prayer times anywhere in the world, with automatic adjustments for Daylight Saving Time.

02

Complete Quran Platform

All 114 Surahs with Arabic text, English translation, transliteration, and high-quality audio recitation from renowned Qaris with verse-by-verse playback.

03

Curated Dua Collection

Over 100 authentic duas organized by category (daily, morning/evening, travel, etc.) with Arabic text, translation, and pronunciation guide.

04

Beautiful Spiritual Design

A calming, elegant interface with Islamic geometric patterns that enhances the spiritual experience while remaining easy to navigate.
