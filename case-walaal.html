Walaal Connect

The Only Somali-First Fundraising Platform

Client Walaal Connect
Year 2025
Services Design & Development
Visit Live Site
01

Project Overview

Walaal Connect is a revolutionary fundraising platform built BY Somalis, FOR Somalis. Unlike traditional platforms like GoFundMe, it enables direct Sadaqah transfers to verified families via mobile money — with absolutely zero fees.

The platform addresses a critical need in the Somali community: connecting diaspora members with families in need back home, while ensuring 100% of donations reach the intended recipients through trusted mobile money services like EVC, Zaad, and Sahal.

0% Platform Fees
100% Direct to Families
24/7 Support Available
02

The Challenge

Traditional Platform Fees

Existing platforms charge 5-10% fees, meaning less money reaches families in need.

Trust & Verification

Donors need assurance that families are verified and funds reach the right people.

Mobile Money Integration

Most Somalis use mobile money, but existing platforms don't support these payment methods.

Cultural Understanding

Platform needed to understand Islamic charity principles and Somali community values.

03

Our Solution

01

Zero-Fee Direct Transfers

We built a system where donors send money directly to families' mobile money accounts. Walaal Connect takes absolutely nothing — 100% of every donation reaches the intended recipient.

02

Rigorous Verification Process

Every family story is verified through local contacts and documentation. Donors can trust that the stories are genuine and the families are truly in need.

03

Mobile Money Integration

Full support for EVC, Zaad, and Sahal — the most popular mobile money services in Somalia. Funds arrive within minutes, not days.

04

Islamic Principles Integration

The platform incorporates Islamic wisdom about charity, with Quranic verses and Hadith to inspire giving and emphasize the spiritual rewards of Sadaqah.
