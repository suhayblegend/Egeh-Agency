UNISOM Admissions

Connecting East African Students with Global Universities

Client UNISOM
Year 2024
Services Full-Stack Development
Visit Live Site
01

Project Overview

UNISOM Admissions is a comprehensive education consultancy platform that bridges the gap between East African students and world-class universities. The platform streamlines the entire admission process, from initial consultation to successful enrollment.

We developed a sophisticated web application that allows students to explore university options, submit applications, track their progress, and connect with experienced education consultants who guide them through every step of their academic journey.

50+ Partner Universities
1000+ Students Placed
95% Success Rate
02

The Challenge

Information Overload

Students struggled to find accurate, up-to-date information about international universities and admission requirements.

Complex Application Process

Multiple document requirements, varying deadlines, and complex procedures made applications overwhelming.

Limited Communication

Students needed real-time support and guidance but lacked direct access to knowledgeable consultants.

Deadline Management

Missing crucial deadlines due to lack of organized tracking systems often cost students their opportunities.

03

Our Solution

01

Comprehensive University Database

We built an extensive database featuring 50+ partner universities with detailed program information, admission requirements, tuition fees, and scholarship opportunities — all searchable and filterable.

02

Streamlined Application Portal

A unified application system allows students to apply to multiple universities from one platform, with document upload, progress tracking, and automated status updates.

03

Consultant Matching System

Intelligent matching connects students with consultants who specialize in their field of study, ensuring personalized guidance throughout the process.

04

Smart Notification System

Automated reminders for deadlines, document submissions, and interview schedules ensure students never miss important dates.
