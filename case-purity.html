Project Overview

Purity Guard is a powerful Chrome extension designed to help Muslims maintain spiritual and digital purity. It combines smart content blocking with beautiful Islamic reminders, creating a comprehensive digital wellness solution.

The extension is completely free, privacy-focused, and built with love for the Ummah. It features advanced filtering algorithms, customizable settings, and unique accountability features like Telegram alerts and Sadaqah commitments.

The Challenge

Digital Age Challenges

Muslims face unprecedented challenges in maintaining spiritual purity in the digital age.

Existing Blockers Lack Soul

Most content blockers focus only on blocking without providing spiritual support or motivation.

Accountability Gap

People need support systems and accountability partners to stay on the right path.

Progress Tracking

Users needed a way to track their journey and stay motivated with streaks and goals.

03

Our Solution

01

Smart Content Blocking

Advanced filtering algorithms with customizable blocklists automatically block harmful websites. Full whitelist and blacklist control.

02

Islamic Reminders

Beautiful Quran verses and Hadith displayed on block pages to strengthen resolve and redirect the soul toward remembrance of Allah.

03

Quran Audio Recitation

High-quality Quran recitations play on the block page to calm the mind and heart, providing instant spiritual comfort.

04

Telegram Accountability

Real-time notifications (1-2 seconds) to your accountability partner when issues arise. You're never alone on this journey.

05

Sadaqah Commitment

Commit to donate to charity when you relapse. Your partner gets proof photos — turning mistakes into good deeds.

06

Progress Tracking

Track blocks, maintain streaks, and set monthly goals to monitor your digital wellness journey.
