Project Overview

Egeh Agency is a bold, modern creative design agency that needed a portfolio website matching their innovative spirit. We designed and developed a striking website that showcases their creative prowess while attracting premium clients.

The website features dynamic animations, immersive project showcases, and a distinctive visual identity that sets Egeh Agency apart in the competitive creative industry. Every element was crafted to reflect their commitment to excellence and innovation.

50+ Projects Showcased

02

The Challenge

Standing Out

The creative industry is crowded. Egeh needed a website that immediately communicated their unique vision.

Showcasing Work

Their diverse portfolio needed an elegant way to present various project types without overwhelming visitors.

Performance & Animation

Complex animations had to be smooth and performant without sacrificing loading speed.

Lead Generation

The website needed to convert visitors into qualified leads and consultation requests.

03

Our Solution

01

Bold Visual Identity

We created a striking orange-accented design with dynamic typography, smooth transitions, and micro-interactions that capture attention and communicate creativity from the first second.

02

Immersive Project Showcases

Each project features its own dedicated page with full-screen imagery, project details, and client testimonials — creating an immersive experience that sells their work effectively.

03

Optimized Animations

Using modern CSS animations and optimized JavaScript, we achieved buttery-smooth 60fps animations while maintaining sub-2-second load times.

04

Strategic CTAs

Carefully placed calls-to-action throughout the site guide visitors toward consultation requests, resulting in a 200% increase in qualified leads.
